# CH03 "The Work" — Image Plan (12 images)

## What these are
CH03 is a horizontal-scroll of **4 service panels**. Each panel has **3 image tiles** stacked on the right in a staggered column (top widest, then indented, then wider again) = **12 images total**. Every tile renders at **4:3 aspect ratio**, dark, with 2px rounded corners.

Each `svc-img` slot carries a placeholder tint that sets a color rhythm down the column — keep it when you grade the real photo so the stagger keeps its music:
- `svc-img-1` → **cold navy** (`#1a1c2a→#1e2535`) — the operator / structure
- `svc-img-2` → **spruce green** (`#1a2218→#1e2a1c`) — grounded / process
- `svc-img-3` → **warm brown/amber** (`#2a1a1a→#2e2015`) — the father / human
- `svc-img-4` → **blue-violet** (`#1a1a2a→#201c30`) — cool / reflective

## House style for all 12 (so they read as one set)
- **Near-black, documentary stills.** One light source per frame. Same one-lamp kit as the film.
- **No clear faces.** Hands, objects, textures, backs, environments — or a face in shadow. Same rule as the film.
- **Amber is scarce.** At most one small warm accent per frame; most tiles are cold or neutral. If amber shows up in every tile, the section loses its power.
- **Film grain**, soft focus falloff, quiet. Nothing staged-looking.
- **Shoot ≥1200×900px** (tiles display up to ~340px but retina + crop room). Compose loose; the 4:3 crop is centered.
- These can be **frame-grabs from the film shoot** or dedicated stills — same room, same lamp, same day.

## PANEL 1 — DISCOVERY CALL  *(Free · 3 questions · honest fit · no pitch)*
Mood: the first honest conversation. Cold entry warming to human.

- **1A — slot `img-1` (cold navy):** A phone lying face-up on a dark desk, screen dim, one small amber reflection on the glass. The call, about to happen. Cold light, wide space around it.
- **1B — slot `img-2` (green):** Overhead of an index card / notebook with **three short ruled lines** (or three faint tick marks) — "three specific questions." Spruce-cool grade, restrained.
- **1C — slot `img-3` (warm):** Two empty chairs angled toward each other in a dark room, one warm lamp between them. The honest sit-down — "he'll tell you." The one warm tile of this panel.

## PANEL 2 — THE RESET  *(4 weeks · clarity · "you leave knowing what you want" · tools)*
Mood: stripping away to the one true thing. Transitional, cool.

- **2A — slot `img-2` (green):** A **cleared desk** — everything pushed to the edges, a single blank sheet in a pool of light. "Knowing is the only place anything useful starts from." Cool green grade.
- **2B — slot `img-4` (blue):** A hand marking a **grid of four** (a calendar, four weekly ticks). Cold, deliberate. "Four weeks."
- **2C — slot `img-1` (navy):** A pen resting on a written page with **one line underlined** — the personalized action plan. Cold-neutral, shallow focus.

## PANEL 3 — THE SHIFT  *(8–10 weeks · momentum · identity, career, fatherhood · discipline)*
Mood: the deepest tier — both worlds at once. This panel is the emotional center; let it be the warmest of the four.

- **3A — slot `img-3` (warm):** The **two-worlds hands rhyme in one frame** — a hand on a work document with a child's shoe (or small hand) at the edge of the desk. Career and fatherhood in the same light. The film's whole thesis as a still. Warm.
- **3B — slot `img-2` (green):** **Momentum** — a figure walking a dark corridor toward a far light, slight motion blur. Back to camera. Spruce grade. "The problem isn't clarity — it's momentum."
- **3C — slot `img-1` (navy):** **Discipline** — ordered objects on a dark surface: a watch, a ledger or a column of numbers, squared edges. Cold. "Financial discipline."

## PANEL 4 — THE RECALIBRATION  *(single session · check your heading · closure · proof the work ended)*
Mood: calm, resolved, nothing left undone.

- **4A — slot `img-4` (blue):** A **single point of light** in near-black — a compass face, or the site's amber diamond motif small and centered. "Need to check their heading." Minimal, cold-calm.
- **4B — slot `img-3` (warm):** A **clean, finished desk** — one warm lamp, surface clear, a closed notebook. "The work had an ending — this is proof of it." Warm, still.
- **4C — slot `img-1` (navy):** A **door left ajar** with faint light beyond, or a hand easing a drawer/door shut. Quiet closure. Cold-neutral.

1C two chairs + lamp · 3A hands + child's shoe · 4B finished desk

## Quality checks
- **Column test:** view each panel's 3 tiles stacked — do the temperatures (cold→green→warm) still step the way the placeholders do? Grade toward each slot's tint.
- **Face test:** no clear face in any of the 12.
- **Amber test:** count the warm accents across all 12 — if more than ~4–5 tiles read warm, pull some back to cold. Scarcity is the point.
- **One-set test:** thumbnail all 12 together — same grain, same darkness, same restraint? They should look shot by one person on one day (because they were).
